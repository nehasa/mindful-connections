Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about getting started and working together.

I typically respond to all inquiries within 24 hours during business days (Monday-Friday). If you contact me over the weekend, I'll get back to you on Monday morning.

During our 20-minute free consultation, we'll discuss your current challenges, your goals for coaching, and determine if we're a good fit for working together. There's no obligation to continue after this call.

Currently, all sessions are conducted online via video call. This allows me to work with clients worldwide and provides flexibility in scheduling. Online sessions are just as effective as in-person sessions.

I accept bank transfers, UPI payments, and major credit cards. Payment plans are available for longer programs. All payments are processed securely.

Each program typically runs for 8-12 weeks, with weekly 60-minute sessions. However, the duration can be adjusted based on your specific needs and progress. We'll discuss the best timeline during your consultation.
