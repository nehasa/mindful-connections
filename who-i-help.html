Who I Help

Happiness depends on 3 things: Luck, Action, and Emotional Strength

The Happiness Framework

True happiness isn't just about chance - it's about understanding and working with these three fundamental elements that shape our life experience.

Understanding the Three Pillars of Happiness

Through my coaching, I help you develop strategies to work with each of these elements, maximizing your potential for lasting happiness and fulfillment.

1. Luck

The circumstances and opportunities that come our way - both favorable and challenging.

How I Help:
  • Recognizing opportunities
  • Making peace with circumstances
  • Finding meaning in challenges
  • Creating favorable conditions
"Luck is what happens when preparation meets opportunity."

2. Action

The choices we make and the steps we take to shape our lives and respond to circumstances.

How I Help:
  • Overcoming procrastination
  • Building confidence
  • Setting meaningful goals
  • Taking inspired action
"The future depends on what you do today."

3. Emotional Strength

The inner resilience and emotional intelligence that helps us navigate life's ups and downs.

How I Help:
  • Building resilience
  • Managing emotions
  • Developing self-compassion
  • Cultivating optimism
"Strength doesn't come from what you can do. It comes from overcoming what you once couldn't."
